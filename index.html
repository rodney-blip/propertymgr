📐 How Numbers Are Calculated ▼

Data Source & Limitations

Properties come from multiple sources:

  • Auction.com (--auction-com mode): Real auction listings from Auction.com via Apify cloud. Addresses, auction prices, dates, and details are real data. Look for the blue "AUCTION.COM" badge. Requires Apify API token.
  • Oregon Sheriff Sales (--sheriff mode): Real courthouse-step judicial foreclosure auctions from oregonsheriffssales.org. Addresses, sale dates, and case parties are real public records. Look for the red "SHERIFF SALE" badge.
  • Redfin MLS (--scrape mode): Real MLS-listed foreclosures and bank-owned properties. Addresses, listing prices, beds/baths/sqft are real data. Look for the green "REAL MLS DATA" badge.
  • ATTOM public records (--real mode): Sale snapshots + property details. Free tier does not return foreclosure flags or mortgage data.
  • BatchData (--real mode): Pre-foreclosure filings (requires paid key).
  • Mock data (--mock mode): Synthetic properties for testing.

For non-Redfin properties:

  • Auction dates are projected 14-60 days out (not from real auction schedules)
  • Foreclosing entity, loan type, stage are modeled from typical market distributions
  • Total debt is estimated at 70-95% of ARV (not from actual lien records)

Upgrading to ATTOM's paid tier ($100-250/mo) or a production BatchData key unlocks real foreclosure filings, mortgage amounts, and seller data.

Estimated Value

For Auction.com listings, sourced from Auction.com's own Est. Resale Value. For other sources, estimated from $/sq ft for the state (e.g. OR $200, TX $160) multiplied by the property's living area. Future: Zillow Zestimate integration.

Max Bid Price (70% Rule)

Based on the 70% rule with a 91% safety cushion:
Max Bid = Est. Value × 0.70 × 0.91
This gives you a ~36% discount from estimated value. Budget for your own repair estimates on top of this. If the listed price is at or below the max bid, the deal has room; if it's above, you'd be overpaying relative to the rule.

Profit Formula

Total Investment = Purchase Price + Closing (3%) + Holding (Est. Value × 1% × 6 mo)
Profit = Est. Value − Total Investment − Selling Costs (Est. Value × 8%)
Note: Repairs are NOT included in this estimate — budget separately after inspection.
Recommended thresholds: margin ≥ 30%, deal score ≥ 60.
